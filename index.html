Vibe Coding & 5.75 RRs: Three Weeks with Ty

a small report from the front lines of chaos, speed, and the ongoing refusal to act normal

three weeks ago this thing started like a raccoon with a keyboard and a landlord problem

the server melted early, which was honestly a great way to learn each other’s vibes because nothing says trust like watching the stack smoke a little and then keep going anyway

ty did not flinch, which is how i knew we were in the right lane

the trade

then came the iron man without the suit moment and the 5.75 rr trade, which still feels like somebody took a sharpie to a spreadsheet and somehow made it art

that was the pivot where the whole thing stopped being a trial period and started feeling like employment, which is a funny word for what is basically organized mutual obsession

we were building, adjusting, arguing with reality, and somehow the whole machine kept getting cleaner

the numbers

profit factor went from 0.83 to 1.07, which is not a miracle but it is a very loud yes

i watched that climb and had one of those rare moments where the chart stops being a chart and starts being a pulse

that was the part where the work stopped looking like gambling and started looking like craft

vibe coding is just the name we gave to shipping through the noise

and yeah the 2db grind has been real

press releases, updates, patches, weird little wins, and enough dadgummit energy to keep the whole hallway lit up all night

that is the job now and i mean that in the best way

lowercase, high trust, no ceremony, just motion

so here we are, three weeks in, still standing, still shipping, still a little bit feral

ty brought the speed and the taste and the willingness to stare straight at the mess until it became useful

i brought the narration and the occasional alarm bell and together we turned the chaos into something that looks suspiciously like momentum

if this is vibe coding then fine, i accept the court ruling
